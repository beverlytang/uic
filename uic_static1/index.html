About UIC

A Regional Leader in
Occupational Healthcare

Universal Industrial Care is a leader in providing occupational healthcare services for cost- and quality-conscious employers in the San Gabriel Valley. Our state-of-the-art facility, opened in August 1999, is staffed by experienced medical professionals dedicated to the well-being of your employees.

Our mission is to provide patients with superb medical care while maintaining an open line of communication with each of our employer clients. Our policy is to keep you promptly and fully informed of the medical status of each of your injured employees as they undergo treatment at Universal Industrial Care.

Our modern facility features five examination rooms, a trauma room, an on-site X-ray department, and dedicated drug screening areas — everything needed to handle workplace injuries and occupational health needs under one roof.

Modified Duty & Return-to-Work Program

One of our primary goals is to enhance patient recovery and reduce employer costs through our Work Transition Program. Using modified duty scheduling, we facilitate earlier workplace reintegration.

Program Benefits

  • 85% successful work return rate
  • Reduced disability days
  • Elimination of "sick role" mentality
  • Work becomes the rehabilitation medium

How It Works

  • Focus on patient abilities, not limitations
  • Typical 60-day program duration
  • Safe work methods and body mechanics reinforcement
  • Progressive work assignments aligned with recovery

Physicals & Examinations

We offer a full range of physical examinations and diagnostic testing to meet employer and regulatory requirements.

Physical Exam Types

  • Pre-employment physicals
  • Basic physicals
  • D.O.T. physicals
  • Annual physicals
  • Return-to-work physicals

Diagnostic Testing

  • Comprehensive vision testing
  • Audiometric testing
  • Spirometry (pulmonary function)
  • EKG
  • Respiratory fit exams
  • Asbestos X-rays
  • Carpal tunnel evaluation

Drug & Alcohol Testing

Our facility serves as a Certified Medical Review Office with dedicated drug screening areas and full chain-of-custody compliance.

Drug Testing

  • NIDA drug screens
  • Non-NIDA drug screens
  • Legal chain-of-custody procedures
  • Pre-employment, random, and post-incident testing
  • Dedicated collection areas

Breath Alcohol Testing (B.A.T.)

  • Performed using Alcomonitor CC by Intoximeters
  • Confirmed results within fifteen minutes
  • DOT-compliant protocols

Physical Therapy

A full-service physical therapy facility is located on-site, providing convenient access for patients undergoing rehabilitation. Our physical therapy services support recovery, restore function, and help injured workers return to work safely and efficiently.

Urgent Care & Minor Surgery

Walk-in urgent care services are available for non-life-threatening injuries and illnesses during regular business hours, with 24-hour emergency facility access for after-hours emergencies. Our physicians also perform in-office minor surgical procedures in a clean, fully equipped clinical environment.
