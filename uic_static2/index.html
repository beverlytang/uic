Who We Are

Committed to Your Workforce's Health

Universal Industrial Care, located in the heart of the San Gabriel Valley, is a leader in providing occupational healthcare services for cost- and quality-conscious employers. We offer full range physical screenings, drug screening services, comprehensive medical treatment for injured workers, and innovative disability management programs.

Our commitment to our clients is to provide patients with superb medical care while keeping an open line of communication with each of our employer clients. We keep you promptly and fully informed of the medical status of each of your injured employees. Our medical professionals are readily available and will respond promptly and completely to your inquiries.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Occupational Healthcare

Return-to-Work Programs

Our Work Transition Program achieves an 85% return-to-work success rate within a typical 60-day program.

  • Reduces disability days
  • Uses actual work as rehabilitation
  • Promotes safe work methods
  • Progressively expanded assignments
