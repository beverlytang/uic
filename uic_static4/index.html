Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Dedicated to protecting and enhancing workforce well-being through quality medical care and employer partnerships.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, compassionate and timely occupational health services that protect and enhance the well-being, safety, and productivity of the workforce. We are committed to delivering quality medical care, promoting injury prevention, and fostering healthier work environments through employer-employee partnerships.

Our Vision

To set the standard for excellence in occupational medicine through evidence-based care and advanced technology, improving workforce health, safety, and productivity. We strive to be the premier one-stop resource for all employer occupational health needs.

What We Offer

Our Services

From physical exams to therapy and drug testing, we provide a full spectrum of occupational health services.

Return-to-Work Programs

Our Work Transition Program uses modified duty schedules to facilitate early workplace reintegration for injured employees, with an 85% successful return-to-work rate.

  • Typical 60-day program duration
  • Uses actual work as a rehabilitation medium
  • Focuses on patient capabilities, not limitations
  • Progressively expands duties matching recovery
  • Frequent physician progress updates to employers
  • Promotes safe work methods and body mechanics
  • Eliminates "sick role" mentality
  • Maintains work routine connection
Physical Examinations

Comprehensive examination services to meet all employer and regulatory requirements:

  • Pre-employment physicals
  • DOT physicals
  • Annual physicals
  • Return-to-work physicals
  • Vision & audiometric testing
  • Spirometry & EKG
  • Respiratory fit exams
  • Asbestos X-rays
  • Carpal tunnel evaluations
Drug & Alcohol Testing

Certified Medical Review Office performing chain-of-custody drug screens with both NIDA and non-NIDA options.

Breath alcohol testing uses the Alcomonitor CC device, providing confirmed results within fifteen minutes. All protocols are NHTSA-approved and DOT-compliant.

On-Site Physical & Occupational Therapy

Our full-service therapy facility is located on-site, providing convenient access to both physical and occupational therapy. This allows seamless coordination between treating physicians and therapists for optimal recovery outcomes.
