Who We Are

Universal Industrial Care, located in the heart of the San Gabriel Valley, is a leader in providing occupational healthcare services for cost- and quality-conscious employers in our geographic region.

Our philosophy is simple: to provide our patients with superb medical care while keeping an open line of communication with each of our employer clients. We maintain a policy to keep employers promptly informed about injured employees' medical status and ensure our medical professionals are always available to respond to inquiries.

Our state-of-the-art facility, opened in August 1999, has five exam rooms, a trauma room, X-ray department, and separate areas for drug screenings.

Our Services

Comprehensive occupational health and workplace services centered around employee wellness and injury recovery.

Work Transition Program

Our flagship program focuses on modified duty and return-to-work initiatives with an 85% success rate. The structured 60-day program emphasizes gradual work reintegration.

Occupational Physicals

Pre-employment, DOT, annual, and return-to-work examinations plus specialized testing including vision, audiometry, spirometry, and EKG.

Drug & Alcohol Screening

Certified MRO services with legal chain-of-custody procedures, NIDA and non-NIDA drug screens, and DOT-compliant breath alcohol testing.

More Services

A complete physical therapy facility operates at our location, providing patients with convenient access to rehabilitation services without the need for offsite referrals. Our therapists work closely with our physicians to create customized recovery plans.
We maintain relationships with medical professionals across multiple disciplines to ensure comprehensive care:
  • General Surgery
  • Internal Medicine
  • Neurology
  • Obstetrics & Gynecology
  • Ophthalmology
  • Orthopedics
  • Physical & Medical Rehabilitation
  • Podiatry
  • Reconstructive Surgery
Our occupational physicals include a wide range of assessments:
  • Pre-employment examinations
  • Basic physical examinations
  • DOT examinations
  • Annual examinations
  • Return-to-work examinations
  • Vision evaluations
  • Audiometry (hearing tests)
  • Spirometry (lung function tests)
  • EKG (heart monitoring)
  • Respiratory fit testing
  • Asbestos-related X-rays
  • Carpal tunnel assessments

Oscar Tuazon, M.D.

Medical Director

Known affectionately by his clients as "Dr. T," Dr. Tuazon ensures that all of his patients receive proper, quality care while maintaining excellent communication with their employers. He has devoted nearly 20 years to industrial medicine, making him a trusted leader in occupational healthcare.
