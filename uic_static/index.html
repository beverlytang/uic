About Our Practice

Dedicated to Superior Occupational Healthcare

Universal Industrial Care is a leader in providing occupational healthcare services for cost- and quality-conscious employers in our geographic region. We offer physical and drug screening services as well as comprehensive medical treatment and innovative disability management programs for injured workers.

Our guiding principle is delivering superb medical care while keeping an open line of communication with each of our employer clients. We are committed to keeping employers promptly and fully informed of the medical status of each injured employee.

Our state-of-the-art facility, opened in August 1999, features five examination rooms, a trauma room, a full x-ray department, and separate drug screening areas — all designed to provide efficient, high-quality care.

Our Services

Comprehensive Occupational Health Solutions

From pre-employment screenings to full rehabilitation programs, we provide the complete spectrum of occupational healthcare services.

Physical Examinations

Comprehensive examination services tailored for workplace requirements and regulatory compliance.

  • Pre-employment physicals
  • DOT examinations
  • Annual & return-to-work physicals
  • Vision & audiometric testing
  • Spirometry & EKG
  • Respiratory fit exams

Drug & Alcohol Testing

Certified Medical Review Office performing compliant screenings with proper chain-of-custody procedures.

  • NIDA & non-NIDA drug screens
  • DOT-compliant breath alcohol testing
  • Chain-of-custody protocols
  • Intoximeters-certified equipment

Specialist Referrals

On-site access to a comprehensive network of board-certified medical specialists.

  • General & orthopedic surgery
  • Internal medicine & neurology
  • Physical rehabilitation
  • Ophthalmology & podiatry
  • Reconstructive surgery

Physical Therapy

Full-service physical therapy facility available on-site, designed to accelerate recovery and restore function for injured workers.

Asbestos & Carpal Tunnel

Specialized diagnostic evaluations including asbestos-exposure x-rays and carpal tunnel assessments for workplace injury claims.

Disability Management

Innovative disability management programs designed to minimize lost work time and control costs while ensuring optimal employee recovery.

Flagship Program

Work Transition Program

Our signature program focuses on a modified duty schedule to return injured employees to work much earlier than traditional programs allow. Through progressive work assignments aligned with recovery progress, we eliminate the "sick role" mentality and reinforce workplace safety.
